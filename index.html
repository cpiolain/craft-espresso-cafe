Our Story

Cafe Espresso is a modern local café located in the heart of Kensington since 2004.

We use locally grown organic produce to serve delicious and healthy breakfast and meals all day, along with our French inspired pastries and 100% organic, fair-trade coffee.

Visit Us! We are open WEEKDAYS 6 a.m. to 11 p.m. / WEEKENDS 7 a.m. to midnight.

18 W 29th Street
